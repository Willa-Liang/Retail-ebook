But adoption succeeds only when three elements align:

01

Organizational readiness

Scale only when it's ready, and only where it matters. Shakey's (Philippines) began AI experiments in 2021-2022, but chose not to scale until the technology matured and could deliver real value. Equally important, business units must tie AI efforts to clear business priorities and measurable outcomes, not just what's possible.

02

Right partnership structure

Business units lead AI adoption in partnership with IT. When business defines problems and IT provides solutions, you get operational impact.

03

Clean foundations

Sanitized data and documented processes prevent AI from amplifying mess. No-layoffs commitments remove fear.
