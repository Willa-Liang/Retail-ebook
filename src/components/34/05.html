AIProcess

AI adoption rarely fails because the technology is absent. It fails because the surrounding workflow is inconsistent, the inputs are weak, or ownership is fragmented.

Teams get better results when they first standardize the handoff, clarify the source of truth, and decide what judgment remains human.

Example: standardize product brief inputs before asking AI to generate variants.

Better prompts start with better operational structure.

Automation scales existing habits. If the habit is messy, the scale simply makes the mess travel faster.

Translation:

AI amplifies process quality. Workflow discipline is the prerequisite, not the afterthought.
